What is a Hairtype?

Hair types can be split into four simple categories; straight, wavy, curly, and coily. These categories help identify the hair type, making it easier to find the proper care and treatment.

As seen in the given diagram; the four categories can also be split into sub-categories, in order to specify even more and specialize regarding treatment. Our site simply works with the four main categories and is able to differienciate based on a simple drawing!
